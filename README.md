#####Body#####

1. The Answer, &c.

    _ The next sort of Arguments respect some special Cases, as (1) of the Transplantation, (2) of the Corporation.

    _ The Guilt of the Corporations in part instanced.

    _ The Guilt of English Protestants and Irish Papists compared.

    _ As to their fighting against the Regicides,

    _ The Arguments following plead Iustice, and that Iustice grounded on the Articles of 1648. and judged by the sad consequences threatned on the breach thereof, whether we regard men or God.

    _ THE CONCLUSION.
